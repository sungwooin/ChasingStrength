Welcome to ChasingStrength

I’m a student who discovered lifting in college. What started as a way to stay fit became my lifestyle. Through discipline and dedication, lifting has kept me focused, built my confidence, and helped me stay away from negative influences. Today, I train others — helping students and working professionals break through physical and mental barriers to achieve their fitness goals.

Fitness is not just about lifting weights; it’s about lifting your entire self. It’s about pushing through when you think you can’t, and coming out stronger on the other side. Body recomposition, fat loss, building strength — it's all about dedication. I believe everyone can achieve their fitness goals, and I'm here to guide you through the journey with custom-tailored workout and meal plans.
