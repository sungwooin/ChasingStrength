About Me & Why I Started

Hey, I’m Prince — the guy behind ChasingStrength. I wasn’t born athletic. In fact, I started out weak, underconfident, and confused about fitness. But I decided to change. I trained, studied, failed, tried again — and built myself from the ground up.

This site is not just a portfolio of what I’ve learned; it’s a hub for you to grow too. My goal is to help students and working individuals — just like you — get stronger, fitter, and more confident through training, without burning a hole in their pocket.

I priced my coaching between ₹100 - ₹1000 because I want it to be affordable. Everyone deserves to feel strong. Let’s chase strength together!

My Fitness Philosophy

Fitness is about more than just appearance. Here’s what we focus on:

  • Consistency: Results come from showing up — day in, day out.
  • Smart Training: Customized workouts that evolve with your progress.
  • Balanced Nutrition: Fuel your body to perform and recover better.
  • Mindset: Build grit, discipline, and confidence that lasts.
