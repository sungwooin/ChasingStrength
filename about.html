About Me

From skinny and weak to strong and confident – here’s my story.

👋 Hey, I’m Prince

I was once the quiet, skinny kid who struggled with strength, confidence, and self-esteem. I used to look at others and wish I had their power, their presence. One day, I decided to stop wishing — and start working. 💪

Through training, proper nutrition, and consistency, I transformed not just my body, but my mindset. I started ChasingStrength to share everything I’ve learned and help others unlock their own potential — especially those who feel like they’re starting from scratch, just like I did.

This site is my way of giving back. Whether you're a beginner or getting back into fitness, I’m here to guide you. My plans are budget-friendly because I know what it feels like to be young and broke — I started this site to help and earn a little pocket money, not to overcharge anyone. 🫱🏽‍🫲🏽

Thank you for being here. Let’s get stronger — together. 🔥
