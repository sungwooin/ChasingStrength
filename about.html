My Fitness Journey 💪

From feeling weak and fragile to becoming stronger and more confident—here’s my story. 📖

2021: The Beginning 🌱

It all started in 2021 when I was determined to change my life. At the time, I was dealing with a number of health challenges, but I knew I had to make a change. My journey began with small, consistent steps towards better health and fitness.

2021: A Setback 🦠

Unfortunately, my health journey was interrupted when I battled dengue 🦠 and malaria 🤒, which left me weak and at 52 kg, with little strength to move forward. But I wasn’t ready to give up just yet!

2022: Resilience and Progress 💥

Despite everything, I picked myself back up. I started training again, and although progress was slow, I remained determined. The ups and downs were real, but so was the belief that if you don’t give up, you’ll eventually reach your goal.

Personal Achievements 🏅

  • 🏆 Overcame health challenges (Dengue, Malaria) to rebuild strength
  • 🥇 Experienced in strength and endurance training
  • 💪 Transformed my own body and mindset through perseverance
  • 🎯 Dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals
